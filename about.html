About Who?

Hi there! I'm Jack Shen, I learned to draw before I could walk, and I've loved code since secondary school. This odd mishmash of interests led me to pursue a B.S. Computer Science at Drexel University.

I'm a UI developer/designer stationed in Philadelphia. I love creating intuitive, eye-catching interfaces. I'm part of Full Moon Studios as a designer and programmer.

Outside of work, I enjoy making art, cheesy fantasy novels, and pretentious teas.
