About Who?

Hi there! I'm Jack Shen, I learned to draw before I could walk, and I've loved code since secondary school. This odd mishmash of interests became a Digital Media major in college.

I'm a UI developer/designer stationed in Philadelphia, and an undergraduate at Drexel. I love intuitive, eye-catching interfaces. I'm part of Full Moon Studios as a designer and programmer

Outside of work, I enjoy creating digital art, cheesy fantasy novels, pretentious writing, and even more pretentious teas.
